Materials Needed

These are the basic items you need to crochet.
  1. Medium weight size 4 100% cotton yarn. Any yarn you have will do!
  2. Size H / 5mm crochet hook. Follow the guidelines on your yarn for hook size. I recommend starting with a larger hook to start until you learn your tension.
  3. Any scissors or shears will work
  4. Large sewing needles. These work great
  5. Stitch markers

A version of all of these items can be found at your local craft store.
